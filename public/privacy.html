PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: August 17, 2025

Preface

At Beige Modeling Management, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients. Any personal or business information you share, such as contact details, booking requirements, or company data, is collected and used solely to provide our services. We do not sell, disclose, or misuse your information. It is shared only with models, staff, or trusted partners directly involved in fulfilling your booking or service request. By using our services, you consent to our data practices outlined below. For any questions, please contact us directly.

User-Provided Details

We collect the following information to facilitate our services:

  • Contact details (name, phone number, email, business address)
  • Company details (business name, position, service needs)
  • Booking information (project details, requirements, measurements, contracts, agreements)
  • Payment and billing information (where applicable)
  • Communications with our agency (emails, phone calls, messages)
  • Other information necessary for effective booking

Information Usage

Your information is used exclusively for professional purposes, such as:

  • Processing bookings and managing contracts
  • Communicating about projects, updates, and services
  • Sending invoices, payment confirmations, or related documents
  • Maintaining accurate business records
  • Improving our services and client-agency relationships

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your information. It may be shared with:

  • Models or service providers involved in your booking
  • Professional partners (e.g., photographers, stylists, event organizers) as needed
  • Legal or regulatory authorities if required by law

Information Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. While we strive for robust security, no system is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

Confidentiality

All client and business information is treated with the highest confidentiality and used only to fulfill professional obligations.

Retention of Data

We retain your information only as long as necessary for legal, financial, or business purposes. Once no longer needed, it is securely deleted.

Your Rights

As a client or partner, you have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your information
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal or contractual obligations
  • Withdraw consent to the use of your information (where applicable)

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be communicated via our official channels, and continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
